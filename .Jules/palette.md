## 2024-05-18 - Contact Form Accessibility Audit
**Learning:** Found a recurring pattern in the design system where form labels are designed without implicit `<label>` wrapping or explicit `id`/`htmlFor` pairings, relying instead on visual proximity and `group-focus-within` styling for user context. This creates a critical accessibility barrier for screen reader users who cannot visually associate the floating label with its adjacent input. Similarly, decorative material icons used inline in buttons are lacking `aria-hidden` attributes, causing them to be read aloud (e.g. "east") confusingly.
**Action:** Always verify explicit `id`/`htmlFor` pairing on custom UI floating labels. Introduce `focus-visible` styling to all custom buttons to ensure keyboard navigation visibility isn't lost in minimal designs.
